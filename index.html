How the Picks Table Works

Rule Details
1. Pick a Team Click the button for the team you think will win the game. Your selection will be highlighted.
2. Assign Confidence Points Use the dropdown menu to assign confidence points (1-14). Higher points mean you're more confident in that pick.
3. Save Your Picks Click the "Submit" button once all your picks and points are assigned. Your picks will be locked after submission.
4. Reset Your Picks You can click "Reset" to clear all your picks and start over, but only before submission.
5. Scoring System You earn points for each correct pick based on the confidence points you assigned. Incorrect picks earn 0 points.
6. Leaderboard Your total score will be displayed on the leaderboard after the games are completed.
