Quick Overview

  • Each week you pick every NFL game and assign confidence points (1–16 or however many games that week).
  • Correct picks earn the points you placed on that game. Highest total wins.
  • There are two views:
    • House Picks — shows everyone’s weekly scores and details.
    • Money Pool — same scoring, but only for people who opted into the pool.

Money Pool

  • Entry fee: $5 per week, per person.
  • The pot = entrants × fee.
  • When the week is finalized, the top score among pool members wins the pot (ties split).
  • Owner fee: 5% of the pot is taken for hosting & maintenance before payout.
  • Net shown on profiles = total won − total staked.

House Picks

  • Everyone’s picks/points are scored here.
  • Top score(s) get a week win badge on their profile.
  • This page is for bragging rights and history; payouts only apply in the Money Pool.

Confidence Points

  • Use each number once (1 = least confident, highest number = most confident).
  • If the team you picked wins, you earn the points you placed on that game.
  • Weeks can have fewer than 16 games; points scale to the number of games that week.

Locking & Fair Play

  • Picks can be edited until lock. Once locked, no edits are allowed.
  • Lock is controlled by the admin and happens at/near the first kickoff.
  • Finalizing results is also done by the admin (after all winners are entered).

Profile & Members

  • Customize your name color, team avatar, and banner on the Profile page.
  • Members shows everyone’s weeks won and net totals.
  • Past Weeks lists historical leaderboards so you can look back.

Costs & Payouts

  • Entry: $5 per person, per week (Money Pool only).
  • Owner fee: 5% of the pot (covers hosting, upkeep, and admin work).
  • Payouts are calculated and posted after the admin finalizes the week.

Example: 12 entrants → pot $60 → owner fee $3 → payout pool $57 → if 1 winner, they get $57; if 3 tied, they split $57.
